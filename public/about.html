Frequently Asked Questions

Are there bank charges on brinmo?

No.
Businesses are only charged 4% of transactions from the customers we bring to them.

Is brinmo safe, is it secure?

Yes.
ATM/Debit/Credit card information is processed by Paystack. They are PCI-DSS certified, licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria, and partners with Visa and Mastercard. They apply the highest standards of card data encryption, so nobody (not us, not even them) can have access.

Every transaction requires the customer's bank to verify their pin and send an OTP(one-time-password).

How are businesses verified?

We visit the business.

How and when do i receive payments?

When our users pay your business, you receive an SMS alert showing that an amount has been paid by a username. You can ask for the customer's username to confirm their identity.

The amount paid is automatically deposited to your bank account by our payment service provider (Paystack).

How do i receive orders and deliver?

First, set your delivery fee and upload items you are ready to deliver.
When a customer orders, you'll receive an SMS Alert of their payment for the item.
That SMS will have the customer's address and phone number for delivery.

How do i add or manage branches?

Create new accounts with different emails for each branch.
Each branch or business must have it's own email account. It will make things easier for the customers, branch managers and operators.

How do i reject orders or return payments?

Contact the Brinmo Support Team immediately, we'll instantly reverse the transaction after an agreement between you and your customer.
